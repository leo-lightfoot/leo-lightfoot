## 🚀 About Me

I'm a **technology-driven finance professional** based in Frankfurt am Main, Germany, combining a **Master of Finance (Frankfurt School)** with a Computer Science background. Currently at **Investment Data Services (Allianz Group)**, I specialize in applying machine learning and quantitative methods to capital markets and financial engineering challenges.

💡 **What I do:**
- Build ML models for financial forecasting (ECB interest rates, portfolio optimization)
- Develop quantitative risk models (VaR, regime-aware allocations, derivatives margining)
- Apply NLP and AI to financial data (sentiment analysis, FinBERT)
- Create interactive financial applications and educational tools

🎓 Master of Finance candidate at Frankfurt School of Finance & Management
